Discover my World

by Luz Alvarado

My name is Luz Alvarado. I was born and raised in Lima, Peru and moved to Vancouver, Canada to pursue my undergrad degree at the Sauder School of Business. I specialized in Marketing and International Business, and did a minor on International Relations. I have a great passion for exploring the world and taking pictures to capture the beauty that surrounds me.

I spent the last half of 2014 in Singapore doing my exchange semester at the National University of Singapore. This experience was life changing as I grew personally and academically. I escaped my comfort zone and challenged myself to live in a part of the world I barely knew about. My communication skills improved, as well as, my ability to work in teams. Also, I expanded my knowledge on brand management and market research through demanding projects.
